About Us

Who are we?

How did this begin?

As group of students, we felt the knowledge we were getting from our University classes wasn't enough.
Under the sugestion and guidance of an older, more experienced colleague, we started this group, the Hackathonners, where in a hackathon-like environment we develop our ideas into working software.

Our Motto

A bunch of people of build things means exactly that. An open group, who wants to learn more by exploring ideas and building them into software.
A bunch of people because we aim to be a big group, whilst for now we are big in talent :).
Building because we belive in a think-before-code approach and also in the fact that creating a piece of software can be as innovating, difficult and laborious as building a new never-before-seen machine or building a 10 floor building.
Things because we are open to all types of software. On one hackathon we might be developing for Android and/or IOS, but on the next we'll be using a No-SQL Database on a web application and the one after that we might be creating a new Chrome Extension.

Our Goal

With this project we aim to:

  • Adquire and share knowledge about differente technologies in the software development world through our hackathon-like events.
  • Create working open-source pieces of/complete software.
  • Learn skills such as team-working, talk presentation and others.
  • Scale our current work model to have hackathons with alot more people or even sponsored events.
